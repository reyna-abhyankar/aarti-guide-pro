bhakta sankati nana swarupi sthapiti swadharma

ambarisha karane garbhawas soshisi
ved nele choruni brahmaya anuniya desi
matsyarupi narayan saptahi sagar dhundisi
hasta tuzha lagta shankasura var deti ||1||

rasatalasi zaata pruthvi paathivar ghesi
paropkarasathi deva kaasav zhalasi
dadhe dharuni pruthvi neta varaharup hosi
pralhadakarane narhari stambhi gurgursi ||2||

paanchvey avtari balichya dwarala zaasi
bhikshey sthal maguni balila patala nesi
sarva samarpan kele mhanuni prasanna tya hosi
wamanrup dharuni balichya dwari tishthasi ||3||

aarti saprem jai jai vitthal parabrahma

sahastraarjun maatla jamadagni cha wadh kela
kashti tey renuka mhanuni sahastraarjun vadhila
nih-kshatri pruthvi daan didhli viprala
sahava avtar parshuram pragatla ||4||

aarti saprem jai jai vitthal parabrahma

maatla raavan sarvaa upadrava kela
tehtees koti dev bandi hariley seeteyla
pitru vachanasathi raamey vanwaas kela
miluni vaanar sena raja ram pragatla ||5||

aarti saprem jai jai vitthal parabrahma

devaki vasudev bandi mochan tva kele
nandaghari zaauni nijasukh gokula didhle
goras chori karita navalaksha gopala milavile
gopikanche prem paahuni srikrishna bhulale ||6||

aarti saprem jai jai vitthal parabrahma

boudhha kalanki kaliyugi adharma ha avagha
soduni didhla dharma mhanuni na disasi deva
mlenchha mardan karisi mhanuni kalanki keshava
bahiravi jaanhavi dyavi nijasukha nandachi seva ||7||
