pranamya shirsa devam
gauri putram vinayakam
bhakta vaasam smare nityam
aayuh kamartha sidhaye ||1||

prathamam vakra tundamcha
ekadantam dwitiyakam
trutiyam krishna pingaksham
gaja vaktram chatur thakam ||2||

lambodaram panchamancha
shashtham vikatamevacha
saptamam vighna rajendram
dhoomra varnam tathashtamam ||3||

navamam bhalchandramcha
dashamam tu vinayakam
ekadasham ganapatim
dwadasham tu gajananam ||4||

dwadashaitani namani
trisandhyam yah pathey naraaha
na cha vighna bhayam tasya
sarva siddhi karah prabho ||5||

vidyarthi labhate vidya
dhanarthi labhate dhanam
putrarthi labhate putra
moksharthi labhate gatim ||6||

japet ganapati stotra
shat bhir maasaihi phalam labhet
sanvatsaren siddhincha
labhate naatra sanshayaha ||7||

astabhyo bramhane bhyashcha
likhitvahi samarpayet
tasya vidya bhavet sarva
ganeshasya prasadataha ||8||

iti shree narad puraane 
sankashta naashanam 
maha ganapati stotra sampoornam ||
